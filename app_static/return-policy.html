Refund

You are eligible for a return and refund within 15 days after delivery. If 15 days have gone by since the delivery of the product, unfortunately we would not offer you a refund.

To initiate a refund, please email us on service@myflyfit.com with your order number. Once we confirm that you are eligible for a refund. We will issue an RMA number and a return form to you. Fill out the form and send the returned product back to the return address.

To be eligible for a full refund, the returned product must be in the same condition that you received it including all the parts and accessories. It must also be in the original packaging. There are certain situations where only partial refunds are granted: Any item not in its original condition, is damaged or missing parts for reasons not due to our error. Please also note that freight expenses and ankle support are not refundable. 

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If you are approved, then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within a certain amount of days.  If you haven't received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.

Replace and Repair

FlyFit warrants that the product is at the time of its original purchase free of defects in materials and workmanship. The Warranty shall last for six (6) months from the date of the original delivery of the product. This Limited Warranty applies only to the hardware components of the product as originally supplied and does not apply to any software or applications.

Before the warranty expires, we replace or repair the product if it is defective or damaged. Contact us at service@myflyfit.com if you find the product defective. We will arrange an online meeting to examine the item.

Once we confirm that the product is defective. We will issue an RMA number and a return form to you. Fill out the form and send the returned product back to the return address. You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. We recommend you to use a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance. We don't guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

After we receive the product, we will examine it and notify you about the result. If the product is not defective, we will send it back to you. If the product is defective. We will send a repaired or replaced one back to you. Depending on where you live, the time it may take for your exchanged product to reach you, may vary.
