  • FlyFit App

    Follow these steps to start using FlyFit for the first time!

    1. Getting Started (download)

      • Download the FlyFIt App to your smartphone

        • Create your FlyFit account

          When you first open Flyfit app, you will be prompted to create a new account either by selecting “Login with Facebook” or “Sign up with Email”. Once creating an account, Flyfit will ask you for your nickname, gender, height and weight. By providing this information, Flyfit will be able to accurately track your calorie burn and other biometrics. Don’t worry, this information will always be kept private.

        • Pair Flyfit with the FlyFit App

          • Bluetooth Setting

            Go into your mobile device’s Settings → Bluetooth → Swipe “On”

            • Tap the Flyfit icon at the top­right corner to pair your flyfit

            • Sync

              • After pairing, Flyfit will automatically sync data with your smartphone or pad each time tapping the Flyfit icon.

                • Multiple syncing within the same day is power consumptive.

                • Battery Life

                  • You can check battery life in the Flyfit info section as long as device is connected.

                    • Device will automatically switches to system sleep to consumpt power.
