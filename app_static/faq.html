  • Service

    • How can I tell if my FlyFit needs help?

      Visit our support site for helpful information. If you have read the articles but still not sure whether your FlyFit needs help, please contact us.

      • How do I get my FlyFit serviced?

        Please contact us, and we will reply to you via e­mail as soon as possible. Alternatively, online meeting could be arranged so that we could examine your FlyFit, but please make an appointment via email first.

        • Hong long does the maintenance take?

          The time required for maintenance usually depends on the nature of the issue and the location you stay. We will notify you how long the maintenance and transportation time would be after we receive your FlyFit.
          Please visit the return and refund site for more information about sending your FlyFit to us.

          • Will my data be preserved during the maintenance?

            All of your data will be preserved online. Hence, you will not have to worry about losing your data during the maintenance.

            • Does the warranty apply to the software or application?

              This limited warranty applies only to the hardware components of the product as originally supplied and does not apply to any software or applications.
